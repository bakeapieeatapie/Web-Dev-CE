History of the sight

SkiSkate.co was born out of a shared passion between a tight-knit crew of skaters and skiers who wanted more than just another shop — they wanted a hub for the community. Legend has it, the idea sparked during a late-night session in a mountain town parking lot, where skis and boards leaned against the same van, and talk turned to how hard it was to find gear and guides that truly got it. So they built Skate&SKi.co — a rider-owned, rider-driven site that caters to every level, from first-time pavement pushers to seasoned backcountry shredders. Whether you're hunting for gear, advice, or the next epic line, Skate&SKi.co is here to hook you up with the knowledge, tools, and vibes to keep you rolling and carving year-round.
